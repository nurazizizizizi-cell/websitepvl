Bahasa Inggris

Understanding about waste is the process where waste (unwanted materials) is managed so that it does not harm health or the environment. Waste management can be done by recycling or reusing the wate into new product. One example is making crafts from cardboard waste, as I have done. This time I made or crafted something from cardboard waste to create a piece of art. In this observasi report task, I will explain the work I made.

Cardboard waste is solod waste from used cardboard that needs to be managed properly so it does not harm the environment. Therefore, there are many useful creations that can be made from cardboard waste, such as table, pencil holders, and other creative ideas. From these ideas, I decided to make a laptop table combined with a book rack.

The concept of the laptop table & book rack I made is as follows: on the table I made, there are holes on each side of the cardboard (left and right). There are four holes in total. For the book rack, I made three compartments connected to the laptop table. For the size, I made it not to big and not to small. I also covered the cardboard craft with flannel fabric and added decorations to make it look more attractive.

From this craft, there are several benefits. Such as the table that can be used for laptop.the four side holes that can be used to store items, and the book rack to keep books. By making this kind of craft, if done at home and reused, it can train our creativity in making a piece of artwork.
